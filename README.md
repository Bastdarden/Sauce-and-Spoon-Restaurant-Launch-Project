# Sauce & Spoon Restaurant Launch & Digital Menu Tablet Initiative

## Project Overview

This project demonstrates the complete project management lifecycle for the planning, implementation, testing, and evaluation of a Digital Menu Tablet Initiative for Sauce & Spoon, a fictional restaurant organization focused on operational excellence, customer satisfaction, and sustainable growth.

Acting as Project Manager, I developed project initiation documents, planning artifacts, implementation schedules, stakeholder communications, pilot testing evaluations, business impact assessments, and project closure reports.

The project showcases practical application of project management principles including project initiation, governance, stakeholder management, schedule management, benefits realization, and project closure.

---

# Business Challenge

Sauce & Spoon experienced rapid growth in annual sales but faced increasing operational challenges, including:

* Longer customer wait times
* Front-of-house employee burnout
* Manual ordering inefficiencies
* Ticketing errors
* Flat profit margins despite increased revenue

To address these challenges, organizational leadership proposed implementing a digital menu tablet solution integrated with existing point-of-sale systems.

---

# Project Objectives

* Reduce food waste by 25%
* Increase product mix sales by 15%
* Reduce table turn times by 30 minutes
* Increase average customer check value to $75
* Improve customer satisfaction
* Support operational scalability through technology adoption
