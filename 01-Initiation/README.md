## Project Proposal

The Project Proposal established the business case for implementing a digital menu tablet solution across Sauce & Spoon restaurant locations. The proposal identified operational challenges related to customer wait times, employee workload, food waste, and profitability, while outlining a technology-driven solution designed to improve efficiency and customer experience.

### Key Contributions

* Developed business case and project justification
* Identified organizational challenges and opportunities
* Defined strategic objectives and success criteria
* Established expected business benefits and ROI drivers
* Presented executive-level recommendations

### Skills Demonstrated

* Project Initiation
* Business Analysis
* Stakeholder Management
* Strategic Planning
* Benefits Realization
* Executive Communication

### Project Charter

The Project Charter formally authorized the Sauce & Spoon Digital Menu Tablet Initiative and established project scope, objectives, governance structure, stakeholder responsibilities, budget constraints, and success criteria. This document served as the foundation for project planning and execution.
